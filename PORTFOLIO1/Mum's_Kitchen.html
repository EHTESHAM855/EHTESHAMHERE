About Us

Mum's Kitchen was founded in 2010 by Jane Smith, a passionate home cook who wanted to share her love of comfort food with the world. With the help of her daughter, Sarah, Jane opened the first Mum's Kitchen restaurant in downtown San Francisco.

Since then, Mum's Kitchen has expanded to multiple locations across the country, but our commitment to serving delicious, homemade meals remains the same. We pride ourselves on using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and creating dishes that remind you of home.

Our Core Values

Fresh and locally sourced ingredients

We believe that using fresh, locally sourced ingredients is not only better for our customers' health, but it also supports our community and helps reduce our carbon footprint.

Extensive selection of premium wines

We offer an extensive selection of premium wines from around the world to complement our menu and enhance our customers' dining experience.

Attentive and personalized service

We strive to provide attentive and personalized service to each of our customers, making them feel welcomed and valued.

Affordable prices without compromising quality

We believe that our customers should not have to choose between quality and affordability. That's why we offer delicious food and drinks at reasonable prices.
